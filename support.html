Common Issues

Not receiving notifications?

  • Check that notifications are enabled in iOS Settings > Sports Alerts.
  • Make sure you're signed in (Settings > Account).
  • Verify your alert preferences are configured (Settings > Alerts).
  • Try sending a test notification from Settings > Debug > Test Push.

Fantasy roster not syncing?

  • Re-authenticate your Yahoo/ESPN/CBS account in Settings > Import.
  • Make sure you've selected the correct league.

App not loading data?

  • Check your internet connection.
  • Try signing out and back in.
  • Force-quit and reopen the app.
