01 · At-a-glance

Every game at a glance, updated live.

The Games tab shows every MLB game today with live scores, current batter and pitcher, ball–strike–out state, and lead direction. Tap any game to drop into the live at-bat.

Postponed, delayed, extra innings, doubleheaders — all correctly labeled without you having to think about it.

02 · Live at-bat

Follow every pitch, live.

Tap into any game and see the live at-bat: pitch-by-pitch strike zone, pitch types and velocity, hit locations, and the field diagram. Refreshes as pitches come in.

Scroll through play-by-play with pitch details on every plate appearance — nothing to click through.

03 · Full box score

Box score without the mess.

Every batter, every pitcher, every stat line — presented cleanly with sortable columns. Switch between innings, plays, box score, and scoring plays without leaving the game.

Live-updating during the game and locked in when it finalizes.

04 · Personal roster

Follow your players.

Add players individually or sync your entire fantasy roster from Yahoo, ESPN, CBS Sports, or Underdog. The app tags each followed player with their fantasy source so you always know which league they're on.

Search across the full MLB player pool — active roster, IL, and minor-league prospects for the callup watch.

05 · Notification history

Every alert, filtered how you want.

Every notification you've received, grouped by game, team, league, or event type. Filter to just injuries. Just callups. Just your fantasy roster. Configurable to your taste.

Tap any alert to jump to the moment it fired in the live game.

06 · Custom templates

Design your notification.

Templates with drag-and-drop tokens — player name, team, source, description, event type. Group alerts, control timing, decide what gets a badge and what stays silent. Every alert comes out looking exactly the way you want.

Sync your fantasy roster.

Connect your league — Sports Alerts auto-follows every player on your team and updates overnight when you drop/add.
