Push notifications, curated for you.

Every alert is tuned to who you follow and how you follow them. No spam, no filler, no generic scoreboard pings.

🎯

Player alerts

Follow any pro baseball player. Get pinged on home runs, extra-base hits, stolen bases, strikeouts, and every at-bat outcome. Notifications include the fantasy league the player is on.

📋

Lineup detection

The moment tonight's lineup is posted, you know. Whether your player is starting, benched, or scratched — plus the opposing pitcher, park factors, and game time.

🚀

Prospect callup AI

Real-time monitoring of beat reporters and insiders. Our classification pipeline flags callups the moment a source breaks it — from any level, any team.

🩹

Injury tracking

In-game exits, IL placements, surgery announcements, return-from-IL activations. Body-part-aware so a hand injury doesn't get lost under a shoulder one.

🔄

Fantasy sync

Auto-import your entire roster from Yahoo, ESPN, CBS Sports, or Underdog Fantasy. When you drop a player and pick up another, alerts update overnight.

🎨

Custom templates

Design how alerts appear. Group by game, team, or event type. Time-sensitive breakthrough delivery, in-app banner controls, drag-and-drop notification bundles.
